Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Subly ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your data when you use our subscription tracking application.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Subly is designed to keep your information private and under your control.

  • Subscription Data – The subscription names, amounts, billing cycles, and payment dates you enter are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • iCloud Sync – If you enable iCloud sync, your subscription data will be stored in your personal iCloud Key-Value Store, which is end-to-end encrypted by Apple and only accessible to you.
  • Analytics & Crash Reports – Subly does not include any analytics SDKs. No usage data, crash reports, or error logs are sent to us.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No account registration or login
  • ❌ No email addresses or phone numbers
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No browsing history or usage patterns
  • ❌ No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • ❌ No third-party analytics

Payments

All payments are processed through Apple's StoreKit system. Subly never receives or stores your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing, receipts, and subscription management through your Apple ID. You can manage and cancel subscriptions at any time in your Apple ID settings.

iCloud Sync

When you enable iCloud sync, your subscription data is stored in your personal iCloud Key-Value Store. This data is:

  • Protected by your Apple ID authentication
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Only accessible to you and devices signed into your Apple ID
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy
For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • Clear All Data – Use the "Clear All Data" button in Settings to delete all subscription information immediately.
  • Uninstall – Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data. If iCloud sync was enabled, the data will also be removed from iCloud.

Third-Party Services

Subly does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app is fully self-contained.

Children's Privacy

Subly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any updates.
