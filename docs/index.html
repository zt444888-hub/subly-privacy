Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Pocket Subly ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All subscription information you enter is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and is never sent to our servers.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud Key-Value Store, encrypted by Apple, and only accessible to you.

Payments

All payments are processed through Apple's StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Clear All Data" button in Settings, or by uninstalling the app.
